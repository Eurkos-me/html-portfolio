Movies and Series

Movies and television are a big part of what I do, as well. A perfect night for me involves grabbing some snacks, planting myself on my couch next to my wife, and watching a story unfold on my TV.

Recent Watches

Fallout (Amazon Prime Video)
I was pleasantly surprised by how well done this series is. It feels so much like the games that, as mentioned above with Fallout 4, it made me want to get back into playing them again. The acting was all top tier, the show is well written and well shot, and the post production was very well done. Definitely check it out if you haven't already.

Night Shift
I wasn't a big horror movie fan until after I met my wife, but she got me into them. This one was pretty good - the pace was a little slow at first and I felt like the twist at the end was a little predictable, but still definitely worth a watch.

X-Men '97
This series is still ongoing (one more episode!), and has been fantastic. The nostalgia hits exactly as it should, but even outside of that aspect it's a great show so far. If you haven't watched any of the old X-Men animated series you'd probably at least want to find a summary somewhere, but don't let that stop you from checking this series out if you're interested.
